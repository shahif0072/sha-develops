Choose between a free subdomain to launch quickly, or invest in a custom domain for a powerful brand identity.

🔗

Subdomain Hosting

A subdomain is a free or low-cost extension of an existing domain. Perfect for startups, portfolios, or testing your web presence before committing to a full domain.

  • Zero annual renewal fees
  • Quick to set up — live in minutes
  • Great for portfolios & personal pages
  • Shared SSL certificate included
  • Ideal for testing & early-stage businesses
  • Free hosting on select platforms
yoursite.netlify.app yourbrand.vercel.app store.myshopify.com
₹0 – ₹500 / year
🌐

Custom Domain

A custom domain like yourbrand.com builds trust, improves SEO rankings, and signals professionalism. This is the gold standard for any serious business.

  • 100% branded URL — your identity
  • Massive trust boost with customers
  • Better Google search rankings (SEO)
  • Custom professional email (you@domain.com)
  • Full ownership — no platform lock-in
  • SSL certificate & security features
yourbrand.com mybusiness.in shopname.co.in
₹800 – ₹2,000 / year

💡 SHA DEVELOPS recommends: For professional businesses, always invest in a custom domain. We help you register, configure and point your domain to your new website — completely hassle-free.
